PowerZone brings a fresh approach to gym membership. By stripping out unnecessary costs (keeping things simple, streamlining our approach and doing away with pushy sales staff) and by focusing on what matters: great equipment in a great space - we aim to reduce costs to you, but deliver a high quality gym experience. We are a fully staffed affordable fitness facility that focuses on offering a unique experience for our members. 1-2-1 personal training, sports rehabilitation, trx group training and group pt sessions are just a few of the exciting services we offer to help you achieve your goals.
At PowerZone we believe everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy a fit and healthy lifestyle. So we have made it simple, affordable & convenient for everyone to achieve their health goals. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, young or old, we're here for everybody.

Imagine an activity that increases your flexibility, strengthens your muscles, centers your thoughts, and relaxes and calms you. Yoga does all that and more! From the moment you walk into the studio, you know you have come to a peaceful place. Our Yoga and Pilates instructors are passionate about what they do ~ teaching from the heart, sharing their years of knowledge, training, and expertise. We practice Pranakriya Yoga, a compassionate yoga, based on the teachings of Swami Kripalu. Founded by Yoganand Michael Carroll, Pranakriya Yoga is a yoga of the heart. Yoga practice is not about learning and mastering more postures, but it is all about flowing and connecting with the breath in each movement and allowing the practice to open your heart and create love for yourself and others.
