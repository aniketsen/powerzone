PowerZone Yoga
Workshops

Yoga workshops at PowerZone are designed with an overall holistic approach to Yoga and Yogic Practices, as they are mainly focussed on imparting the ancient knowledge as mentioned in the scriptures, which constitutes the theoretical aspect of all the asanas and other yogic practices that we know today.

We, at PowerZone, believe that knowing the inner significance of the yogic poses and their effects on various parts of our body is as important as physically practicing them, as it gives an individual the power to make a conscious choice as to which yogic practice would be most beneficial to him/her depending on his/her body constituency or what we call 'boundary conditions' in engineering terms.

Yoga workshops with some specific agenda are usually conducted during weekends. Normally they are 3-10 hour program for one day or spread across 2 weekends. Workshop will be on some key topics and are designed in such a way that it can be integrated in to one’s lifestyle easily. We even conduct many workshops specific to some specific ailments. These workshop can be conducted at our Yoga institute in Bangalore or at other locations depending on the requirement and availability of our teacher. The workshop can be customized based on the interest and requirement.

Target audience:
1. Those living far from the center to attend our regular classes
2. Those who are busy during weekdays to commit for a scheduled time
3. Those who want access to quality scientific/logical teaching of Yoga.

'Introduction to Yoga' Workshop

This Introduction to Yoga workshop is designed for beginners who don’t have any prior experience of Yoga and for experienced Yoga practitioners who are interested to explore the fundamentals in greater detail.

Along with overview of Yoga, the workshop will introduce the participants to the science of body alignment, importance of right posture, benefits of Yoga practice and sneak peak in to the practice of yoga. We have a very scientific approach so as to appeal to a logical mind.

This is a 90 minutes Yoga workshop which includes 45 minutes of theory and 45 minutes of practice.

The goals of the Yoga workshop:

1. Enable one to make an informed decision about pursuing Yoga.
2. Set right foundation for Yoga practice and level set every practitioner who intends to practice at PowerZone.
3. Creating awareness as to how and why Yoga streamlines the biomechanics of the human body.


Target audience:

1. Beginners of Yoga practice
2. Experienced Yoga practitioners who are keen to know more about body alignment
3. Those who want access to quality scientific/logical teaching of Yoga.

Introduction workshop is mandatory for all those who willing to join group classes.

PowerZone Yoga
Retreats

Want to spend your weekend with yourself? Imagine spending your weekend learning yoga to rejuvenate yourself!
The ‘PowerZone Yoga Retreat’ is a perfect way to learn yoga, and relax during your weekends amidst serene nature outside Bangalore or at our center near Nandi Hills. This 2-days retreat is targeted to working professionals who have limited time and who are willing to experience and incorporate yoga into their daily lives. Yoga retreat will cover many aspects of Yoga such as asanas, pranayama, mediation, etc. The classes will be for longer duration but it will not be physically exhausting as the retreat will cover lot of theory in order to make one understand the practice in greater detail. You can always schedule occasional private classes with us later on to clarify doubts and deepen the knowledge. Materials required for self-practice will be provided at the end of the retreat.

Target audience:
1. Working professionals who want to learn yoga but are unable to make time for long duration courses
2. Beginners who need initial push to practice yoga
3. Those who want to learn basics of yoga to start practicing on their own
4. Those who want to understand their postures better, so that they can correct it to get rid of chronic pains
5. Those who want to rejuvenate their body and mind
6. This is designed for beginners and intermediate practitioners
Note: In the interest of giving proper individual attention for all students, not more than 20 students will be allowed per batch.
