PowerZone Yoga Classes

Yoga courses at PowerZone are designed in the respect that, our courses adopt the precepts from the science of Vedanta and Vedic literature to tune the body and the mind in the harmony that it was born with. First of all, the participants are being given lessons on strengthening the body and then the mind and spirit as well through different postures of asanas and breathing techniques (Pranayama). This makes the body more immune to diseases and more strong and sturdy. It certainly increases the strain bearing capacity and stress resilience along with building a nervous system made of steel. It definitely increases courage, determination, creativity, mental and physical hygiene making a positive contribution in all aspects of life, be it health, wealth, relationships, peace and prosperity.

PowerZone offers regular yoga courses in different aspects of Yoga and initiating one on the path of Ashtanga Marg of Yoga as per Pathanjali Yoga Sutras and the ancient science of Vedas. As Yoga is an ancient science and there have been few styles and evolutions that it went through, PowerZone also teaches and professes those schools of thoughts as well.

JOIN US TO LEARN AND PRACTICE THE MOST TRADITIONAL AND AUTHENTIC STYLES OF YOGA. GAIN KNOWLEDGE AND EXPERTISE FROM THE BEST YOGA TEACHERS.
